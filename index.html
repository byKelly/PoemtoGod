In These Lands

Draw my sword
For the Lord

Stand in the fight
For what is right
Let God's might
Spread like light

Feeling His hands
Upon these lands



Giving Glory to God


Give glory to God
The only one
Who has risen
His only Son

Give thanks to Him
For dying on a tree
Loving us so much
He set us all free


Meal Prayer

Let's take our seat
In order to eat

Giving thanks to the Lord
For all that He does provide
His love ever so wide





All I Have



All I have to give
Is for the Lord
All I have to live
Is for my Lord
All I have to say
Is for the Lord
Each and every day
Is for the Lord
Loving him with praise
Is for the Lord
Now and always
Is for the Lord






Poetry by Princess Kelly
